Course Overview

An Economics Department typically focuses on the study of how societies use resources, make decisions, and manage wealth and income. Economics is divided into two main branches: microeconomics, which examines individual and business decision-making, and macroeconomics, which looks at broader economic factors like inflation, unemployment, and national policies.
